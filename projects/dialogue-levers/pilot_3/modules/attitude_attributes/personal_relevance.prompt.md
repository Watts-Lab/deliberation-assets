---
type: multipleChoice
notes: |
  Construct: Personal Relevance — "the extent to which people believe
  that a topic or attitude object holds significant consequences for
  some aspect of their lives" (Petty & Krosnick 2014).

  Wording adapted to distinguish direct life-impact from emotional
  reaction. The literature has relatively few standardized formats
  for this construct; we deliberately reworded to address two known
  ambiguities: (a) "affects you personally" can read as emotional
  impact, and (b) some attitude objects don't have an explicit
  outcome (e.g., "Is the ACLU good for America?") but still affect
  the respondent directly. The "direct impact on your life" framing
  handles both cases.

  See README.md > Personal Relevance for alternative phrasings
  considered.
---
### To what extent does this topic have a direct impact on your life?

---
- 0: Does not affect my life at all
- 0.25: Affects me slightly
- 0.5: Affects me a moderate amount
- 0.75: Affects me a lot
- 1: Has an extreme effect on my life
