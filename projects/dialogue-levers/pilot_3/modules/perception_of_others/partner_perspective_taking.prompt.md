  Upstream JSON field: `partnerPerspectiveTaking`. Positively-valenced —
  higher is better partner ability to take the participant's view.

  This is the PARTNER-direction perspective-taking item: how well could
  THEY see the participant's POV. Pairs with `self_perspective_taking`
  (how easily the participant could take their POV) for a bidirectional
  read on empathy.

  Note the scale is different from `self_perspective_taking`: that one
  asks about subjective ease ("difficult" → "easy"); this asks about
  observed success ("not at all" → "perfectly"). The two are
  intentionally not commensurable: ease ≠ skill.
---
