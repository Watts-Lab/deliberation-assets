## What you'll do

At the start of the conversation, you and your partner will **each answer one question from the list below**:

- When was the last time you walked for more than an hour? Where did you go?
- How did you celebrate last Halloween?
- Do you like to get up early or stay up late? Why?
- If you could have any one superpower, what would it be and why?
- What was your favorite meal as a child?

## Why we’re asking you to do this

Beginning with a simple warmup question gives you an easy way to start your conversation before turning to the main topic.

## What to expect

At the beginning of the call, you’ll each pick **one** of the warm-up questions to answer.

After about 3–4 minutes, you’ll shift into a normal back-and-forth conversation about the assigned topic.
